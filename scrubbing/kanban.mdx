---
title: "Kanban board"
description: "Visual workflow board for managing job scrubbing status."
---

The Kanban board at `/scrubbing` displays all active scrubbing jobs organized by status columns. It uses the `@hello-pangea/dnd` library for drag-and-drop functionality.

## Layout

The board displays columns for each active status (terminal statuses are hidden by default):

```
in_field | action_required | needs_scrubbing | recurring_review | scrubbing | ready_for_review | changes_required | client_approval | ready_to_bill
```

Each job card shows:
- Job title and Jobber ID
- Client name
- Current assignee (if claimed)
- Time in current status
- Comment count
- Priority indicators

## Interactions

### Claiming a job

Click on a job in the `needs_scrubbing` column to claim it. This moves it to `scrubbing` and assigns it to you.

### Submitting for review

From the job detail view, click "Submit for review" to move the job to `ready_for_review`. You can add review notes with the submission.

### Adding comments

Comments can be added to any job regardless of status. Comments are typed as `note`, `review`, or `status_change`.

### Drag and drop

Jobs can be dragged between columns when the transition is valid. Invalid drops are rejected and the card snaps back to its original position.

## Filtering

The board supports filtering by:
- Client name
- Assigned scrubber
- Status
- Date range
- Jobs with comments vs. uncommented
