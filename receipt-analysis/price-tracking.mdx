---
title: "Price tracking"
description: "Historical price analysis across vendors for cost optimization."
---

The price tracker (`lib/price-tracker.ts`) maintains a history of prices for matched products across vendors. This enables cost comparison and identifies pricing trends.

## How it works

When a receipt item is matched to a canonical product, the price tracker records:
- The vendor
- The unit price
- The date
- The receipt reference

This builds a historical price database in the `vendor_prices` table.

## Vendor comparison

For each product, the tracker can show:
- Current price by vendor
- Historical price trend per vendor
- Best current price across all vendors
- Price change alerts (significant increases or decreases)

## Use cases

- **Cost optimization**: Identify which vendor offers the best price for each product
- **Budget forecasting**: Track price trends to predict future costs
- **Anomaly detection**: Flag receipts where prices are significantly different from historical averages
- **Bulk purchasing decisions**: Compare per-unit costs across package sizes and vendors
