---
title: "Bulk optimizer"
description: "Batch pricing analysis across receipts for cost savings."
---

The bulk optimizer component at `/receipt-analysis` analyzes pricing across multiple receipts to identify cost-saving opportunities.

## Features

### Cross-receipt price comparison

Compare the same product across multiple receipts and vendors to find:
- The best per-unit price available
- Volume discount opportunities
- Vendor-specific pricing patterns

### Batch analysis

Upload multiple receipts at once and the optimizer:
- Groups identical or similar items across receipts
- Calculates total spend by product and vendor
- Highlights items where switching vendors would save money
- Estimates potential savings

## API endpoint

```
POST /api/receipt-analysis/optimize
```

The optimize endpoint accepts filters for date range, vendor, and product category, and returns a report of pricing opportunities.

## When to use

Run the bulk optimizer:
- After processing a batch of monthly receipts
- When evaluating a new vendor relationship
- During quarterly spend reviews
- When stocking recommendations change significantly
