The stocking categories module (`lib/stocking-categories.ts`) defines a hierarchical product taxonomy used to organize extracted receipt items and stocking recommendations.

## Category hierarchy

Items are organized into categories by the type of work they support. The taxonomy includes categories such as:

- **Plumbing** — pipes, fittings, valves, fixtures
- **Electrical** — wire, switches, outlets, breakers
- **Irrigation** — sprinkler heads, controllers, pipe, valves
- **General maintenance** — fasteners, adhesives, cleaning supplies
- **Safety** — PPE, signage, first aid
- **Tools** — hand tools, power tools, consumable accessories

## How categories are assigned

Categories are assigned during the [normalization](/receipt-analysis/normalizer) step:

1. UPC-matched products inherit the category from the canonical product record
2. Semantically matched products inherit the category from the matched canonical product
3. Unmatched products are assigned a category by the AI extractor based on the description

## Category management

New categories can be added to the taxonomy in `lib/stocking-categories.ts`. Each category includes:
- A unique identifier
- A display name
- A parent category (for hierarchy)
- Keywords for automatic assignment
